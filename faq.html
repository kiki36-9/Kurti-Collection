Frequently Asked Questions

What is Kurti Collection?

Kurti Collection is an online store offering a wide range of stylish and high-quality Kurtis for all occasions. We aim to provide the best products at affordable prices.

How can I order a Kurti?

Simply visit the 'Shop' section, choose the Kurti you like, and add it to your cart. Then, proceed to checkout to complete your order.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to select countries. You can check the available shipping options at checkout.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number to track your order on our website or through the shipping provider.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Please visit our 'Return Policy' page for more details and instructions on how to return items.
